Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior robotics experience?

Basic programming knowledge is helpful, but we'll teach everything from fundamentals to advanced concepts. Bring enthusiasm and curiosity.

What if I don't have a laptop?

Limited laptops are available on request. Indicate your need in the registration form and we'll coordinate.

How are seats allocated?

Seats are first-come, first-served with a review to ensure a diverse mix. Early registration is strongly recommended.

Is lunch provided?

Yes. Lunch and refreshments are included. Please mention any dietary restrictions in your registration.

Can I keep the robotics kit?

Kits are for use during the workshop only. You'll receive guidance and resources to build your own afterward.
