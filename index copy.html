About the Program

Project ARC is an intensive, hands-on robotics and control systems course designed for students aged 17 and above who are passionate about the future of technology. Our program combines cutting-edge theoretical knowledge with practical application.

Whether you're interested in mechanical engineering, computer science, or electronics, Project ARC provides the comprehensive foundation you need to excel in robotics and automation.
